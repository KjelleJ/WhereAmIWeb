Where am I? – 1.0

The Where am I app finds your way. The app works with GPS and OpenStreetMap. Use the functions Where am I or I am lost possibly in combination with the Mark function.

Where am I?

Touch Where am I and your current location is shown on the map as a red cross. Additional info like latitude, longitude, accuracy, and altitude may also be displayed. See also Set Mark below.

I am lost – Show Direction!

I am lost is intended to be used when you are totally lost. Touch I am lost – Show direction! and start walking. The map is oriented in the direction you walk. Your route is shown on the map. Touch the Stop button when you are sure about the correct direction.

Set Mark

Set Mark sets a mark (a finish flag 🏁) on the map. You can set the mark where you are or on another place. From Where am I and I am lost the “crow path” from your current position to the mark is shown as a dashed line on the map.

Mark here may be useful for example when you want to go back to your car — set the mark before leaving the car.

Mark on another place sets the finish flag on any place on the map. Enter the name of the place (for example an address). The place is shown on the map and you can tap the map to move it. Then press Save (💾).

To remove the mark, touch the Mark button and confirm Remove Mark.

When using the mark you can run Where am I and I am lost a number of times and check that you are heading towards the finish flag.
